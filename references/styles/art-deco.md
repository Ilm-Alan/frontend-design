# Art Deco / Geometric

## Identity

Art Deco — symmetry, geometric ornament, metallic accents, Jazz Age opulence. 1920s–30s visual language translated to screen. Descends from the Chrysler Building, Erté's fashion plates, A.M. Cassandre's posters, Jean Dupas murals, Miami Beach hotel lobbies, The Great Gatsby covers.

The core move: centered symmetry, geometric ornament, deep saturated base with a metallic accent. Deco earns its gold.

## Variants

- **Classical Deco** — Gold on deep navy or oxblood, fan motifs, vertical emphasis, serif display caps in ornate form. 1925 Paris Exposition aesthetic.
- **Streamline Moderne** — Rounded corners, horizontal emphasis, chrome and speed lines, teardrop shapes. Art Deco's aerodynamic 1930s phase — trains, ocean liners, radios.
- **Gatsby / Jazz Age** — High ornament, gold leaf feel, ornate frames, crowned typography, champagne-and-velvet sensibility. Hotel and theater branding 1920–1935.
- **Miami Deco** — Pastel Deco — seafoam, coral, cream with chrome accents. South Beach 1930s–40s.
- **Neo-Deco** — Deco geometry in a modern palette, deco shapes with minimalist restraint. Current luxury hospitality brands, select fragrance houses.

## Typefaces to Reach For

**Display (Deco serif):** Bodoni (at display sizes), Didot, Thorne, Broadway, Peignot, ITC Anna, Glamour, Metropolis (period authentic), Primer.

**Display (Deco sans-serif):** Bernhard Gothic, Bifur, Parisian, Nobel, Poiret One (contemporary Deco-revival), Aquiline, CA Deco.

**Body:** Bodoni Moda, Didot, Baskerville, Sabon — elegant serifs carry Deco body copy.

**Crowned caps / letter-spaced caps** — a hallmark Deco move. Display set in all-caps with `letter-spacing: 0.15em` to `0.3em`, often with a custom serif.

**Metallic gradient stroked lettering** — SVG text with linear gradient fill (gold palette: `#D4AF37 → #F9E8A8 → #AF8A42`) over a dark base.

## Palette Moves

- **Classical base:** Deep saturated — navy (`#0A1A3C`), oxblood (`#4A1820`), forest (`#0D3822`), aubergine (`#2D1B3D`), ink black (`#0B0A0E`).
- **Gold / brass:** `#C9A96E`, `#AF8A42`, `#D4AF37`, `#BFA06A`. The primary metallic — gold should appear as a thin stroke, a border, a sparse accent, not a flood.
- **Chrome / silver:** `#C0C0C0`, `#A0A0A5`, `#E5E4E2` for Streamline Moderne variant.
- **Cream / ivory highlight:** `#F2EAD3`, `#F8F1DD` — ornament text color, contrast against the deep base.
- **Miami Deco pastels:** seafoam (`#9FC8C8`), coral (`#FF9999`), cream (`#FFF8E7`), chrome accent.
- **Dark mode:** Deeper versions of the same palette — Deco is already largely dark. `#04081A` navy, `#2D0E13` oxblood; gold stays consistent.

## Composition Moves

- **Symmetrical compositions** — centered vertical axis is the baseline. Balanced around a central element (monogram, headline, ornamental figure).
- **Fan motifs, sunburst patterns** — SVG radiating lines from a focal point, often behind headings or in hero backgrounds.
- **Stepped / ziggurat geometry** — nested rectangles with progressive insets, inspired by skyscraper setbacks (Chrysler Building).
- **Ornamental borders** — custom `border-image` with Deco patterns, or SVG frames with fan corners, geometric roundels.
- **Vertical ornament runners** — decorative columns of pattern running down page sides.
- **Letter-spaced all-caps** (`letter-spacing: 0.2em`, `text-transform: uppercase`) for section dividers and subheads.
- **Metallic gradient strokes** on type via SVG or `-webkit-text-stroke` plus `background-clip: text`.
- **Monogram marks** — intertwined letterforms as logotype or ornament.
- **Decorative line breaks** — not just a rule, but an ornament: lozenge, fan, stepped diamond.

## Motion Flavor

Stately and unhurried. 400–600 ms eases, `cubic-bezier(0.45, 0, 0.15, 1)`. Reveals unfurl vertically (symmetrical expansion from center). Shimmer effects on gold accents — subtle gradient animation cycling slowly. Fan / sunburst animations on accent elements — 3–5 second cycles. Symmetry preserved in motion: if an element enters from the left, its counterpart enters from the right on the same timing.

## Depth & Texture

Dimensional ornament without literal skeuomorphism. Subtle emboss on type (`text-shadow: 0 1px 0 rgba(0,0,0,0.4)` for a carved-in-stone feel). Metallic surfaces via `linear-gradient` + `background-clip: text`. Drop shadows are soft and deep — think stage lighting on a gilded frame. Film grain appropriate for period feel. Velvet or marble textures (subtle, tiled) for hero backgrounds in the most opulent Deco variants.
